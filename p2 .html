ABOUT THE FACULTY

The Faculty of Engineering at Shubra is one of the well-known engineering faculties in Egypt.
It is part of Benha University and is located in Shubra, Cairo.
The faculty offers a variety of engineering programs such as civil, electrical, and mechanical engineering.
It is known for its strong academic curriculum, practical training, and focus on preparing students for real-world engineering challenges.
Many graduates from this faculty have successful careers in different engineering fields both in Egypt and abroad.
